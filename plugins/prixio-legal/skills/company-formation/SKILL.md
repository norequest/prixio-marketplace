> **Last updated**: March 2026.

## LLC Registration Process

### Step 1: Prepare Documents

**Required documents:**
1. **Application form** (available at House of Justice or online)
2. **Charter/Articles of Association** (წესდება) — can use standard template
3. **Founders' identification** — passport or Georgian ID for each founder
4. **Power of Attorney** (if using representative) — notarized
5. **Address confirmation** — legal address in Georgia (can be virtual office)

**For foreign founders additionally:**
- Notarized passport translation (Georgian)
- Apostilled Power of Attorney (if not present in person)

### Step 2: Register at House of Justice

**Where**: Any House of Justice (იუსტიციის სახლი) or online via napr.gov.ge

**Processing options:**

| Speed | Timeline | Fee (GEL) |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Standard | 1 business day | 100 |
| Expedited (same day) | Same day | 200 |
| Online registration | 1 business day | 100 |

**What you get:**
- Unique Identification Number (ს/კ — საიდენტიფიკაციო კოდი)
- Extract from the Entrepreneurs Registry (ამონაწერი)
- Automatic registration with Revenue Service (rs.ge)

### Step 3: Post-Registration Steps

1. **Open a bank account** at any Georgian bank (TBC, BOG, Liberty, etc.)
   - Need: Extract, charter, director's ID, tax ID
   - Timeline: Same day at most banks

2. **Register on rs.ge** — happens automatically but verify access:
   - Go to rs.ge → log in with Electronic Signature or ID card
   - Verify company details are correct
   - Set up authorized representatives

3. **Get Electronic Signature** (ელექტრონული ხელმოწერა):
   - Required for rs.ge filings
   - Available from certified providers
   - USB token or mobile-based options

4. **Apply for special status** (if applicable):
   - VZP → LEPL Financial-Analytical Service (10 working days)
   - IC Status → Revenue Service application
   - GITA Startup → gita.gov.ge application

5. **Register for VAT** (if needed):
   - Mandatory when turnover exceeds 100,000 GEL in 12 months
   - Voluntary registration available anytime
   - Done through rs.ge portal

## Individual Entrepreneur (IE) Registration

### Best for freelance developers, solo IT consultants

**Process:**
1. Visit House of Justice with Georgian ID or passport
2. Fill application form
3. Fee: **20 GEL** (standard) or **40 GEL** (expedited)
4. Registration: **1 business day** (standard) or **same day** (expedited)

**Post-registration:**
1. Apply for **Small Business Status** at Revenue Service (rs.ge)
   - Online application
   - Processing: 1-3 business days
   - Gets you the 1% turnover tax rate
2. Open bank account (personal or business)
3. Begin monthly declarations on rs.ge

## Foreign Founder Considerations

### No Restrictions on Foreign Ownership
- Georgia does **not restrict** foreign ownership in IT companies
- 100% foreign-owned LLCs are common
- No minimum investment requirement
- No prior approval needed from government

### Restricted Sectors (NOT applicable to IT)
- Air traffic control
- Railway systems
- Defense production
- Nuclear energy
- Currency minting

### Entry and Residence
- Citizens of 95+ countries can enter Georgia **visa-free** for 1 year
- For longer stays: residence permit options
  - **IT Residence Permit** (new Sept 2025): 3 years, renewable 3 times (total 12 years)
    - Covers: IT employees, freelancers, company executives + family members
    - Includes: small business owners in IT, remote IT workers
  - **Investment Residence Permit**: requires investment threshold
  - **Work Residence Permit**: standard employee permit

### Mandatory Work Permits (from March 1, 2026)
- All foreign workers must obtain **"right to labor/entrepreneurial activity" permit** from Ministry
- Review takes up to **30 calendar days**
- Must be obtained **before** starting work or applying for D1 visa/residence permit
- **Penalties**: 2,000 GEL first offense (both worker AND employer), doubles for repeat within 1 year, triples thereafter
- **Transition period**: foreigners already in labor migration system by March 1, 2026 have until **January 1, 2027** to obtain permits
- Visa-free entry (1 year) for 95+ countries remains for visiting, but NOT for working

## Post-Formation Ongoing Obligations

### Monthly
- File declarations on rs.ge (even if zero activity)
- Pay salary tax + pension for employees (by 15th of following month)

### Annually
- Submit annual profit tax declaration (by April 1)
- Submit financial statements to SARAS (reportal.ge)
- File transfer pricing documentation (if applicable)

### As Needed
- Register for VAT when exceeding 100,000 GEL threshold
- Update registry for any changes (address, director, charter amendments)
- Apply for/maintain special status certifications
