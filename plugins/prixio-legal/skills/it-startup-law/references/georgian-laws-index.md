## Key Articles Reference

### Tax Code Articles for IT
- **Art. 8** — Definition of Virtual Zone Person
- **Art. 23¹** — International Company Status
- **Art. 88-90** — Small Business Status (IE)
- **Art. 98¹** — IC tax rates
- **Art. 99¹(p)** — VZP profit tax exemption
- **Art. 130** — Dividend withholding (5%)
- **Art. 206** — Withholding tax procedures

### Copyright Law Articles for Software
- **Art. 5(1)** — Copyright applies to creative works
- **Art. 6(1)** — Computer programs are protected literary works
- **Art. 16** — Works for hire (employer IP rights)

### VZP Law Articles
- **Art. 2** — Definition: IT = development yielding software products
- **Art. 3** — Tax Code governs VZP taxation
- **Art. 4** — VZPs exempt from special regulations/licenses
- **Art. 5** — Status granted within 10 working days
