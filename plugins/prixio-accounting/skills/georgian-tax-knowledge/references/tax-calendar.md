# Georgian Tax Calendar — Prixio LLC

## Monthly Deadlines (Every Month)

| Deadline | Form | Obligation | Condition |
|----------|------|------------|-----------|
| 15th | III-07 | Withholding tax declaration (salary income tax 20%) | If employees exist |
| 15th | III-09 | Pension contribution declaration (2% + 2%) | If employees exist |
| 15th | III-10 | VAT declaration (18%) | If VAT-registered |
| 15th | III-04 | Corporate profit tax (15%) + dividend withholding (5%) | If profit distributed |
| 15th | — | Payment of all declared taxes | If any declaration filed |
| 15th | — | Zero declarations for all marked types with no activity | If marked on info card |

## Annual Deadlines

| Deadline | Obligation | Who |
|----------|------------|-----|
| March 31 | Individual entrepreneur income tax return | IE only (not LLC) |
| April 1 | NAPR annual registration update | All entities |
| October 1 | SARAS financial report submission | Category I–IV entities |

## Penalty Calendar

| Violation | Penalty | Accumulates |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Late/missed declaration | 100 GEL | Per month |
| Late tax payment | 0.05% per day | Daily on unpaid balance |
| VAT non-registration past threshold | Retroactive VAT + penalties | From threshold date |
