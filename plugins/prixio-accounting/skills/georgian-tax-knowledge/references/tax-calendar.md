> **Last updated**: March 2026

| Form | Obligation | Condition |
|------|------------|-----------|

| — | Payment of all declared taxes | If any declaration filed |

| — | Small business tax payment (1%/3%) | If small business status |

## Penalty Calendar

| Violation | Penalty | Accumulates |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Late/missed declaration | 200 GEL first time; 400 GEL repeat in same year | Per occurrence |
| Late tax payment | 0.05% per day (~18.25% per annum) | Daily on unpaid balance |
| Tax understatement | 50% of understated amount | One-time |
| VAT non-registration past threshold | 5,000 GEL + retroactive VAT + penalties | From threshold date |
| Failure to issue tax invoice | 10% of transaction value | Per transaction |
| Tax evasion (intentional) | 100% of evaded amount + criminal liability | One-time |
| Late SARAS filing | Administrative fines | Per occurrence |

## Pension Contribution Details

| Contributor | Rate | Cap |
|------------|------|-----|
| Employee | 2% of gross salary | No cap |
| Employer | 2% of gross salary | No cap |
| State | 2% of gross salary | Only on salary ≤24,000 GEL/yr |
| State (reduced) | 1% of gross salary | Salary 24,000–60,000 GEL/yr |
| State | 0% | Salary >60,000 GEL/yr |

Mandatory for employees who are Georgian citizens/permanent residents.
Self-employed: voluntary (2% + 2% government match).
